ISNB Logistics - Refrigerated Transport & Logistics Solution

Who we are

ISNB Logistics is one of the fastest growing service providers in the refrigerated transporting sector. Operating in Melbourne, ISNB provides refrigerated transportation on a pan-Australia network and refrigerated warehousing services that are capable of handling diverse array of temperature-sensitive products according to your temperature requirements.

the ISNB brand

Set up in 2018, ISNB Logistics Pty Ltd is a pioneer in the refrigerated logistics sector with a vision to provide a wide selection of different customised supply chain solutions based on our customers constantly changing needs. Together with our partners, we can offer you a variety of services catered to your specific requirements. It is mentored and led by a team of professionals who collectively have umpteen years of experience in logistics. It is a self-funded company presently focusing on the intra-city mile distribution business and specialising in refrigerated transport services across major cities in Australia.
